Vision

At Kingdom Life Ministries, we believe that a life lived well puts Christ first. Our vision is to reach the lost by embracing God’s love, empower with the truth of the Word, and disciple so we can all grow and serve together.

What WE BELIEVE

WE BELIEVE The scriptures inspired.

The Bible is the inspired Word of God, a revelation from God to man, the infallible rule of faith and conduct, and is superior to conscience and reason, but not contrary to reason (2 Timothy 3:15, 2 Timothy 3:16, 1 Peter 2:2).

Leadership

Senior Pastor

John and Heather Garcia

Pastor Johnny gave his life to the Lord in 2008. Since then, he has grown spiritually and answered the calling on his life. He has a heart for God’s people.

Johnny and his wife Heather met in 2004 in Knoxville, TN. They moved back to Johnny's hometown Kingston, NY in 2008. They've been happily married since 2012. They worked together as youth leaders for many years. Johnny and Heather are the proud parents of four daughters: Arielle, Alleyah, Alexis, and Madison.

Assistant Pastor

Sara and Jonathan Carle

Jonathan and Sara have always felt the call of servanthood. Since giving their lives to the Lord, they have seen Him work in them in amazing ways. Sara was born on the small Caribbean Island of Montserrat. As she has walked with the Lord, she was given opportunities to serve in varying capacities.

Jonathan has served with Pastor Johnny and his wife Heather as youth group leaders. Jonathan and Sara have been married for over 10 years and are proud parents of three wonderful children. They reside in West Hurley.
